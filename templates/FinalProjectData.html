Data Sources

Data of games played by Magnus Carlsen is available for free in PGN format on chess.com. I used it to create my own dataset.

Data Manipulation

Then, I manipulated the data to obtain a dataset focused on Magnus' moves.

Variables used to train the model

• Elo of the opponent
• Pace of play
• Time remaining for Magnus
• Time remaining of the opponent
• Position evaluation (white/black advantage in centipawns)
• Quality of the move played by Magnus (best move, inaccuracy, error, blunder)
• Quality of the move played by the opponent
• Target variable: Time spent by Magnus to play his move
